All about me:

I am a self taught developer that builds web sites that target your audience and increase conversion rates. By targeting your intended audience your site will be easy to navigate allowing your visitor to find what they are looking for right on your homepage. This means your visitors are engaged and don't leave your site.

Each site is built so that it will:

  • Target your intended audience
  • Provide an exceptional user experience
  • Offer higher conversion rates
  • Increase online engagement

Every web site I build uses a responsive design concept which is a mobile first design strategy. This means that the web site can be viewed on a multitude of different devices and screen sizes. As most people surf the web from their smartphones this means that they will get the full experience as if they were using their desktop

Why Use me? I create quality web sites using techniques and technologies that are current and proven to work.
